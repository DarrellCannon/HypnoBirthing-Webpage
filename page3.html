About Me

When I first found out I was pregnant with my daughter, my first child, I was awash with emotions; surprise mostly, followed by elation and joy. It didn't take me long before I started thinking about the birth and I quickly realised I did not want the traumatic birthing experience that was so frequently depicted in the media, on the soaps that I (much to my husband's annoyance!) love to watch, and that many women around me, throughout my life, had told me about.

Hypnobirthing is Empowering

After doing lots of reading I decided a Hypnobirthing was the way I would get the comfortable birthing experience I wanted.

At my first class, my husband went from being extremely cynical to a huge advocate and now, anytime anyone mentions pregnancy he always talks about our Hypnobirthing experience!

Hypnobirthing is Natural

I could go on and on about my birthing experiences and I just love to talk about them. This is a common trend I’ve noticed with women and their birthing companions who do Hypnobirthing®.

I really do look forward to hearing from you and helping you on your way to having the comfortable birthing experience that you deserve!
